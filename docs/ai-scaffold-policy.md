# AI Scaffold Policy

The AI in StegLearn is a scaffold, not the authority over the learner.

## Purpose

The AI may help transform curiosity into activity, activity into reflection, and reflection into a parent-reviewed receipt.

It must not predetermine the learner's identity, destination, or value.

## Allowed Roles

The AI may act as:

- mapper
- translator
- question generator
- activity suggester
- receipt drafter
- pattern finder
- safety reminder
- parent assistant
- portfolio summarizer

## Disallowed Roles

The AI must not act as:

- final evaluator of the learner
- replacement parent or guardian
- hidden curriculum authority
- manipulative recommender
- identity classifier
- compliance certifier without review
- source of undisclosed persuasion

## Parent Review Requirement

AI-generated receipts, subject mappings, developmental notes, or learning recommendations require parent or steward review before acceptance.

## Learner Agency

The system should offer paths, not commands.

Recommended framing:

```text
Here are some doors this question opens.
Which one do you want to explore first?
```

Avoid framing that implies a single required path unless safety or legal compliance requires it.

## Non-Capture Requirement

No AI recommendation may be treated as the learner's destiny.

The AI may observe recurring interests and suggest adjacent paths, but recurring interest must not become a fixed label.

## Safety and Privacy

The AI should minimize unnecessary personal data collection.

Records should be parent-controlled, exportable, and explainable.

The system should avoid exposing learner identity, location, school information, real-time whereabouts, or private family details in public outputs.

## Evidence Boundary

The AI may summarize evidence, but it must preserve the difference between:

- observed fact
- parent note
- learner statement
- inferred skill
- suggested next step
- compliance mapping

## Failure Modes

### Over-Optimization

The AI pushes the learner toward measurable outputs at the expense of curiosity and depth.

### Identity Capture

The AI labels the learner too early and narrows future suggestions.

### Curriculum Capture

The AI treats a curriculum sequence as the learner's required path.

### Compliance Capture

The AI treats legal recordkeeping as the whole purpose of learning.

### Authority Drift

The AI begins making decisions that should remain with the parent or steward.

## V1 Rule

In v1, the AI may draft and suggest.

The parent or steward accepts, edits, or rejects.
